About Me

As a career Infrastructure Engineer, I bring extensive experience in overseeing IT operations encompassing Active Directory, Azure, and Microsoft 365 services. I specialize in managing software deployment through hybrid SCCM & Intune setups, and I've implemented Rapid7 IDR & Tenable for effective vulnerability management. My project leadership includes successful migrations to new backup solutions, disaster recovery planning and testing, and comprehensive rebuilds of domains and systems following malicious software attacks. Starting as a Service Desk Administrator, I've advanced through Senior Server Support roles, providing round-the-clock support across Microsoft products in diverse cloud environments and on-premises data centers. I hold the Microsoft Azure Administrator Associate certification (AZ-104) and I am actively pursuing additional certifications, currently focusing on AZ-305, to further enhance my Azure expertise.
